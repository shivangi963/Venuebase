Venuebase
Facility & AV Reference Sheet
Last updated: March 2024 | Contact: ops@luminavenues.com

------------------------------------------------------------
MAIN BALLROOM
------------------------------------------------------------
Capacity varies depending on setup:
  - Rounds (banquet): 500 guests
  - Classroom rows: 350
  - Theater/auditorium: up to 600
  - Cocktail/standing: ~700 (no tables)

Floor area is roughly 12,000 sq ft. Ceiling clearance is 24 ft
at center, drops slightly near the east wall due to HVAC
ducting — worth flagging if clients need rigging on that side.

------------------------------------------------------------
AV SETUP
------------------------------------------------------------
Projection:
  4x ceiling-mounted 4K laser projectors (20k lumens each)
  2x 180" motorized screens — one front, one rear-projection

Sound:
  Bose line array, installed 2022. Covers the full room
  including the pre-function corridor. 12 wireless channels
  available (mix of lapel + handheld). Clients bringing their
  own band or production team should coordinate with our AV
  vendor (contact provided on request).

Connectivity at podium: HDMI, USB-C, DisplayPort. Adapters
kept at the AV desk — presenters don't need to bring their own.

------------------------------------------------------------
INTERNET / WIFI
------------------------------------------------------------
1 Gbps fiber line dedicated to attendees. Separate 500 Mbps
line for staff/presenter backstage use. Both are on different
SSIDs. IT support is on-site during event hours.

Wi-Fi 6 APs in every breakout room. Passwords reset per event.
Secured/hidden SSID available for sensitive corporate sessions
on request.

Note: streaming to external platforms (Zoom, Teams, etc.)
works fine on the attendee line. Large file transfers during
the event should go through the staff line.

------------------------------------------------------------
BREAKOUT ROOMS
------------------------------------------------------------
6 rooms total. Capacity ranges from 20 to 40 depending on
layout. Each has a 65" 4K display, whiteboard wall, and its
own climate control. Rooms 3 and 4 can be combined via
partition if needed (combined cap ~70 classroom style).

------------------------------------------------------------
STAGE + LIGHTING
------------------------------------------------------------
48 ft wide modular stage. LED floor is standard. Full DMX
rig — our lighting tech programs it in advance, changes
during the event cost extra. Green room is backstage with
a preview monitor.
