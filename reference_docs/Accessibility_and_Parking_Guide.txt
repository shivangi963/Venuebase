Accessibility & Parking Info
Facilities Team | Last updated: Oct 2023

------------------------------------------------------------
ACCESSIBILITY
------------------------------------------------------------
All properties are ADA compliant. Ramps at every entrance/
exit. Multi-floor venues have elevators with braille buttons.

Restrooms: accessible stalls with grab bars on every floor.
Turning radius meets ADA spec — checked during last
facilities audit.

Main ballroom has a hearing loop (induction loop) installed —
useful for attendees with hearing aids. Wasn't in the original
build, was added in 2021. Works well, worth mentioning to
clients with accessibility-conscious groups.

Wheelchair seating: reserved areas in all event spaces with
clear sightlines to the stage. Locations marked on the
floorplan, available from the events team.

------------------------------------------------------------
PARKING
------------------------------------------------------------
Valet: complimentary for up to 200 vehicles. Anything over
that needs to be flagged in advance — we use an overflow lot
nearby but it requires coordination.

Self-park: 500-space structure on site, complimentary for
guests. Accessible spots (20 minimum) are right next to
the main entrance.

------------------------------------------------------------
GETTING HERE
------------------------------------------------------------
About 2 miles from the central metro station. Ride-share
drop-off zones are marked at the main entrance — Uber/Lyft
drivers seem to find it fine.

------------------------------------------------------------
GROUND TRANSPORTATION
------------------------------------------------------------
We have a partnership with a licensed shuttle company.
Hotel-to-venue shuttles can be arranged at extra cost.
Contact events@luminavenues.com to get a quote and
coordinate timing.

Note: we do not provide airport transportation directly.
Clients asking about airport pickups should be directed
to the ground transport partner via the events team.

------------------------------------------------------------
SERVICE ANIMALS
------------------------------------------------------------
Welcome throughout the property. Relief area is near the
main entrance — marked on the site map.
