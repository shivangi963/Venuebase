Venuebase
Cancellation, Rescheduling & Insurance Terms
Contracts Team | v2.3 — Updated Feb 2024

------------------------------------------------------------
CANCELLATION
------------------------------------------------------------
90+ days before event:   full refund of deposits
31–89 days before:       50% refund of total contracted amount
30 days or fewer:        no refund, full amount due

All cancellations must be in writing to contracts@luminavenues.com.
Verbal notice does not start the clock.

Force majeure: reviewed individually. We've handled these
reasonably in the past but we don't guarantee anything in
advance — refer client to the contracts team directly.

------------------------------------------------------------
RESCHEDULING
------------------------------------------------------------
One free reschedule if requested 60+ days before the event.
Within 60 days: 15% rescheduling fee on the total contract.

------------------------------------------------------------
INSURANCE — CLIENT REQUIREMENTS
------------------------------------------------------------
Clients must carry at least $2,000,000 general liability.
Proof of insurance due 30 days before the event.
Venuebase must be listed as additional insured.

If the event includes alcohol service, an additional liquor
liability rider of $1,000,000 minimum is required. This is
non-negotiable and comes up during contract review.

------------------------------------------------------------
OUR COVERAGE + INDEMNIFICATION
------------------------------------------------------------
Venuebase holds a $5,000,000 venue liability policy.
Clients are responsible for their guests' actions and agree
to hold us harmless from related claims.

------------------------------------------------------------
DAMAGE DEPOSIT
------------------------------------------------------------
$5,000 refundable deposit required for all events. Returned
within 14 business days after the event if there's no damage.
