Catering & Dietary Policy
F&B Team | Revised: Jan 2024

This doc covers what we do and don't allow on the food side.
Sales team — please read the outside vendor section carefully
before promising anything to clients.

------------------------------------------------------------
ALLERGEN / KITCHEN SETUP
------------------------------------------------------------
All kitchens on property are nut-free certified. This is not
just a preference — it's a hard certification we maintain
through bi-annual third-party audits. Same goes for shellfish
cross-contamination.

Dedicated prep stations are used for allergen-sensitive meals.
These are not shared with the main line during events.

------------------------------------------------------------
DIETARY OPTIONS
------------------------------------------------------------
Vegan: available across all meal periods — breakfast, lunch,
dinner, breaks. No need for advance notice, these are built
into our standard menus.

Gluten-free: available but needs 14 days notice minimum so
we can source appropriately and adjust kitchen prep.

Kosher / Halal: we work with two certified vendors for this.
21 days notice required. Client pays the vendor directly —
we coordinate the logistics.

------------------------------------------------------------
OUTSIDE CATERING
------------------------------------------------------------
Not allowed. Full stop. No external food vendors or caterers
on premises. This protects our allergen certifications and
frankly keeps the kitchen running smoothly during events.

The one exception: specialty cakes or desserts from an
approved external vendor. This needs pre-approval from the
F&B manager minimum 30 days out. If a client asks about this,
loop in the F&B team before confirming anything.

------------------------------------------------------------
BAR SERVICE
------------------------------------------------------------
Full bar with licensed bartenders is available. Non-alcoholic
packages also available. All service follows local licensing.

------------------------------------------------------------
MINIMUM SPEND
------------------------------------------------------------
Full-day corporate events: $75 per person per day catering
minimum. This applies regardless of how many meal periods
they take.
